DistriVote Distributed Voting Contract as a Service

Create Carbon Vote
Carbon Vote conduct voting with number of voter's coins without requiring coins to leave voter's wallet.
Create Normal Vote
Normal Vote conduct voting with number of voters as decision criteria.
Trusted and community reviewed voting dapp to ease vote creations backed by your organisation tokens or number of users.

Built using IPFS and Ethereum

Contract code: https://github.com/parag-arora/distrivote-caas
